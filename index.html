Taylor Dupuy

Associate Professor
Department of Mathematics & Statistics, University of Vermont
taylor dot dupuy at gmail dot com

Spending the 2025–2026 academic year at the GAATI Laboratory. Office: Innovation E439.

Before UVM I was a postdoc of Ehud Hrushovski, and a student of Alexandru Buium. My work tends to revolve around Diophantine equations, differential algebraic geometry, deformation theory, Witt vectors and the Frobenius, absolute geometry, applied model theory, and abelian varieties. I'm happy to talk to anyone about any kind of math; send me an email.
